Enhanced Head Football - Test Build

New Features Implemented

1. Goalposts with Physics

  • White crossbar at top of each goal
  • White vertical posts on sides
  • Ball bounces naturally off posts and crossbar
  • Goals can still be scored through the opening

2. Advanced Shooting Mechanics

  • Kick power increased to 900 (was 500)
  • Ball ALWAYS bounces when kicked, even from ground
  • Directional shooting based on player-ball angle
  • Special leg kick power: 1000 velocity

3. Animated Player Legs

  • Visible leg graphics for each player
  • Leg animates forward when kick button pressed
  • 200ms kick animation duration
  • Leg reach: 35 pixels from player center
  • Circular hitbox at foot for ball detection

4. Enhanced Ball Physics

  • Ball bounces away when trapped between players
  • Improved bounce on player contact (300 force)
  • No more "dead ball" situations
  • Natural physics with goalposts

Controls

Player 1 (Red): WASD to move, W to jump, Space to kick

Player 2 (Blue): Arrow keys to move, Up to jump, Shift to kick

ESC: Pause game
